Short Bio

I am a senior undergraduate student at Department of Computer Science, Zhejiang University. I am currently working on spatio-temporal data mining and visualization under the supervision of Prof. Yingcai Wu, State Key Lab of CAD&CG. During the summer of 2016, I worked under the supervision of Prof. Wei Wang at UCLA on text mining and topic modeling. My research interests include data mining, machine learning and information visualization.
