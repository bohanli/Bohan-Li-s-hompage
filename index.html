Short Bio

I am a graduate student at the Carnegie Mellon University Language Technology Institute. Previously, I received my B.Eng degree in Computer Science from Zhejiang University in 2017. During my college years, I was honored to work on various topics in machine learning and data mining with Dr. Lei Li at Toutiao AI Lab, Dr. Yu Zheng at Microsoft Research, Prof. Wei Wang at UCLA and Prof. Yingcai Wu at ZJU State Key Lab of CAD&CG. My current research interests lie in machine learning and natural language processing.
